# HR Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee demographics, incomes, attrition, and workforce distribution to help HR departments understand employee behavior and retention.

---
## Dataset

IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset
From Kaggle.com

---
## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX

---
## Data Cleaning

- Removed constant columns.
- Renamed columns.
- Improved data types.

---
## DAX Measures

- Total Employees
- Average Monthly Income
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate %
- Average Age
- Average Years at Company

---
## Dashboard Features

- Employee Distribution
- Attrition Analysis
- Income Analysis
- Age Distribution
- Department Analysis
- Gender Distribution

---
## Business Insights

- Sales department has the largest workforce.
- Attrition is higher among employees with lower tenure.
- Research & Development has the highest employee count.
